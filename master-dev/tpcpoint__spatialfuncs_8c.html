Detailed Description

Instant constructor and per-point spatial predicates for the tpcpoint temporal type.

Provides the three entry-points needed for per-event streaming (e.g. MobilityNebula NES operators): tpointcloudinst_make, nad_tpcpoint_geo, and eintersects_tpcpoint_geo.
