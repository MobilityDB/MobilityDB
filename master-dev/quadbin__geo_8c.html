Detailed Description

Geometry adapters between a QUADBIN cell and PostGIS geometries.

These typed wrappers keep the geometry construction inside MEOS so the SQL cell/geometry conversions are pure catalog projections (the PG V1 wrappers in mobilitydb/src/quadbin/quadbin_ops.c are thin). The pure cell kernel meos/src/quadbin/quadbin.c stays free of any geometry dependency; the lon/lat coupling lives here, mirroring the h3 split between h3index.c and th3index_latlng.c.
