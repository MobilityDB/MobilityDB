Detailed Description

Polygon Boolean clipping for temporal-point spatial functions.

Thin trivial-cases layer over the Clipper2 adapter (clipper2_clip_poly_poly in clip_clipper2.cpp). The empty-input and bbox-disjoint short-circuits handled here are the same ones the legacy Martinez implementation used; they let the SQL wrappers avoid paying the Clipper2 setup cost on trivially-empty intersections, which is the dominant case during temporal-point clipping over a long trajectory restricted to a small AOI.
