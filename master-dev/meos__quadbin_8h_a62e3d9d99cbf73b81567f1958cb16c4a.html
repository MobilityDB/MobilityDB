typedef uint64 Quadbin

A CARTO quadbin cell index, packed into a 64-bit integer.

Quadbin is a square quadtree Discrete Global Grid System: a Web-Mercator (EPSG:3857) slippy-tile (z, x, y) encoded as a single uint64. Resolution (zoom) ranges 0..26. This is the square/aperture-4 counterpart of the hexagonal/aperture-7 H3Index.
