char * quadbin_cell_to_quadkey ( Quadbin  cell)

Return the canonical base-4 quadkey string of a quadbin cell.

The quadkey is the slippy-tile identifier: one base-4 digit per zoom level, from coarsest to finest. Digit d at a level packs the tile column bit in d & 1 and the tile row bit in d & 2. A zoom-0 cell has the empty quadkey.

Parameters
[in]cellQuadbin cell
Returns
