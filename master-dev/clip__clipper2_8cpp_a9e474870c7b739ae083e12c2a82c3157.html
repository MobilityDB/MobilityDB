Convert an LWGEOM (POLYGON or MULTIPOLYGON) to a flat Paths64 set.

All rings — exteriors and holes, across every component polygon — are appended into the same Paths64. With FillRule::EvenOdd this correctly reproduces the source topology because LWGEOM holes are topological (geometrically contained), not signed-area.
