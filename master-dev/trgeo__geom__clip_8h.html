Detailed Description

Swept-edge-polygon clip primitive (M1 + M2)

Computes time intervals during which a moving polygon edge intersects a static polygon. The swept envelope is a parallelogram (M1, pure translation) or a curved quadrilateral (M2, with rotation).

Note: "trapezoid" in this codebase refers to the shape swept by a tcbuffer segment (trapezoid_make). The trgeo swept quadrilateral is a distinct geometric object.

Sibling of tpoint_geom_clip.c, which handles the static-edge × polygon case. The two will share a unified Edge / point-in-polygon infrastructure once both wire into the build.
