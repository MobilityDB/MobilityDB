Subtype-dispatching wrapper used by both tgeompoint and tgeogpoint entrypoints.

Every path validates the lon/lat SRID through geo_to_h3index_cell (the instant adapter, the non-densify branch, and the first lookup of the densify walker), so non lon/lat input is rejected before any cell is produced and the dispatcher itself needs no separate guard.
