57 * Datum packing
58 *
59 * H3Index is a uint64, binary-identical to int8 in PG's Datum
60 * representation on 64-bit platforms. These macros hide the int64
61 * round-trip so call sites read as H3Index-typed.
62 *****************************************************************************/
