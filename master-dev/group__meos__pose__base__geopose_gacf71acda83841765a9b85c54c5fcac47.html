◆ pose_yaw()

double pose_yaw ( const Pose *  pose)

Return the yaw angle of a pose, in radians.

For a 2D pose this is the stored rotation theta, which is by convention the yaw of the body frame. For a 3D pose this is the Z component of the (yaw, pitch, roll) ZYX intrinsic Tait-Bryan decomposition of the orientation quaternion (the convention required by OGC GeoPose).
