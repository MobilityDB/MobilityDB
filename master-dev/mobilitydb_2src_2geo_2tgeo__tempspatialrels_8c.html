Detailed Description

Temporal spatial relationships for temporal geos.

These relationships are applied at each instant and result in a temporal Boolean.

Depending on the parameters various relationships are available

  • For a temporal geometry geo and a geometry: tcontains, tdisjoint, tintersects, ttouches, and tdwithin.
  • For two temporal geometries: tdisjoint, tintersects, tdwithin.
  • For two temporal geographies: tdisjoint, tintersects.
