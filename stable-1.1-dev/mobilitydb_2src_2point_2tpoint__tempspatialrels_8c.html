Detailed Description

Temporal spatial relationships for temporal points These relationships are applied at each instant and result in a temporal Boolean.

Depending on the parameters various relationships are available

  • For a temporal geometry point and a geometry: tcontains, tdisjoint, tintersects, ttouches, and tdwithin.
  • For two temporal geometry points: tdisjoint, tintersects, tdwithin.
  • For two temporal geography points: tdisjoint, tintersects.
